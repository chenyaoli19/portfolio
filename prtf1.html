SnapTrip

A trip planning app



This is an iPhone app providing users with one application to flawlessly plan, book, and share their trips. Built to employ the exponential growth of the social media driven web and mobile device applications, the app allows users to “snap” hotels, restaurants, attractions, etc to user’s books, in-turn saving them time in planning, pricing and securing travel.


I worked as the UI/UX designer on this app.
